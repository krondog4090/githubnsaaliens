1 - 60

Peacebloom, Silverleaf, and Earthroot

Once you have trained Mining in your respective starting zone you can start leveling Mining straight away. Collect any Peacebloom and Silverleaf you come across. I've found that Elwynn Forest tends to have the most efficient leveling route out of the starting zones. However, that isn't always the case depending on the server which is why I will be providing the most efficient leveling routes below.

Elwynn Forest
